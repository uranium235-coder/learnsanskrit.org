More on idam

Here we will finish our study of the pronoun idam. The pronoun is highly irregular and uses different stems for each gender.

Note the following:

  • The dual forms in cases 3 through 7 are identical to ayam's dual forms.
  • The plural forms in cases 3 through 7 are almost identical to ayam's plural forms. The difference is just in the first vowel, which is e for the masculine and ā for the feminine.
